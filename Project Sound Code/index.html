WHAT IS SOUNDCODE?

Sound Code is a Ministry application that will aid the identification of any digital audio/sound released in and by the ministry. This technology uses audio fingerprinting to enable users easily index/search audio/video materials with short recordings. This solution is solely targeted for Our Ministry and Ministry Content.
Our ministry has over 1000+ digital content of Pastor's messages, ministry songs and other published audio/video contents. With this large amount of content, the only means of identification of any material is by their titles. We are working to make it easier, with an internet enabled device, SoundCode will listen and bring up relevant information.

INSPIRATION & MOTIVATION

So we've had times where we hear a really wonderful song and wanted to know if it was done by LoveWorld Singers or we hear a really inspiring message and we want to know the name of the message but it becomes a problem because we don't know the name. So we thought what if you could get a song or message with just a recording clip of 10 seconds or less? With just less than 10 seconds of the audio you get every information associated with the message currently playing or song. Wonerful yes?
At CLC 2021, Our Man Of God charged us to use innovation strategically as a tool in our warfare. The Sound Code exists as a fulfilment of that mandate as it influences people to learn to worship God rightly in spirit and truth and enables them to live built up by the Word of God.
